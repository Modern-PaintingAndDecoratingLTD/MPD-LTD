About Us

We are Modern Painting and Decorating

We are MP&D LTD, a painting and decoration company. Although recently created, each employee has a minimum of 10 years experience in painting and decorating.

With the rapid pace of growth that we have secured quality of performance, we have already been able to complete several challenging, such as time and quality of the site, and record clients such as Rolls Royce, Chemring Counter Measures Ltd, Grief, Ferrybridge Power Station, and others in our portfolio.

The activity of the company extends throughout the territory of the UK. The services we offer are accompanied by our professionalism and excellent customer service.

Our Goal

Our goal at MP&D is to give you as clients reasonably priced, fast, and most importantly, quality services. Always supports you as a customer while having an individual approach, which aims at your satisfaction with the final results.
